Our Studio

Forty Years of Building Pakistan

Majed Khan & Associates was established in 1985 as a firm of consulting architects, engineers, town planners, and interior designers. Over four decades, the practice has completed more than 500 projects — from individual residences to 2,000-acre master plans.

The firm's work spans commercial, religious, hospitality, health, education, transport, and industrial sectors across Pakistan and beyond. MKA has provided professional services to the National Highway Authority, Pizza Hut Pakistan, Defence Housing Authority, Lahore American School, the Hashoo Group, and numerous public and private sector clients.

Based at 98-E Model Town, Lahore, and in practice since 1985, MKA operates under the leadership of Abdul Majed Khan and Muhammad Akbar, both long-standing members of Pakistan's professional architectural and engineering bodies.
